Basic Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies

1 sticks of butter
1/2 c. sugar
1-1/4 c. brown sugar
2 eggs
2 t. vanilla extract
4 T. milk
1 t. baking soda
1/2 t. salt (optional)
1-3/4 c. all purpose flour
2-1/2 c. rolled oats
1 bag of chocolate chips (Ghirardelli Dark Chocolate are best)

Instructions:
1. Pre-heat oven to 370F
2. Soften butter by mixing with flat beater of stand mixer
3. Add sugars to butter and beat for 1 minute
4. Add eggs and vanilla and mix on medium speed until well incorporated
5. Mix flour, baking soda and salt in separate bowl.
5. Add all dry ingredients except chocolate chips and mix on low speed until well incorporated. Scrape sides of bowl as necessary
6. Add chocolate chips and mix until incorporated

Drop spoonfuls onto cookie sheet, leaving at least 1.5" room between

Bake for 13 minutes or until lightly browned around edges, and then transfer cookies to cooling rack
